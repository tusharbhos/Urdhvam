About Us

30+ years of excellence

Urdhvam is a Pune-based environmental technology company offering sustainable solutions for India’s water crisis.

Our Mission

To develop innovative, cost-effective, and scalable products to improve groundwater sustainability.

Our Vision

To build a climate-resilient and secure world through sustainability, innovation, and excellence.

Why Choose Us

At Urdhvam, we offer smart, sustainable, and affordable solutions to India’s groundwater crisis. Our patented BoreCharger technology revives dry borewells without civil work, increasing recharge by up to 20×. With 3,500+ successful installations, we combine science, innovation, and impact to ensure reliable water access and long-term groundwater sustainability.
