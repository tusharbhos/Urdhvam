About Urdhvam

At Urdhvam Environmental Technologies Pvt. Ltd. (UETPL), we are on a mission to redefine groundwater sustainability in India and beyond. Headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, UETPL is a purpose-driven environmental solutions company committed to solving one of India’s most pressing natural resource challenges—groundwater depletion.

We bring together the power of innovation, technology, and geoscientific excellence to develop affordable, scalable, and field-proven solutions for groundwater rejuvenation and sustainable water management.

  • Flagship Innovation: BoreCharger
  • Beyond BoreCharger: Comprehensive Groundwater Solutions

Why Choose Us

To develop and deploy affordable, scalable, and scientific solutions that address India’s critical groundwater challenges and empower institutions and individuals with knowledge, tools, and technologies for sustainable water management.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is BoreCharger and how does it work?

    BoreCharger is an innovative groundwater recharge solution that rejuvenates borewells by cleaning and enhancing water storage capacity. It uses a combination of mechanical cleaning and sustainable recharge techniques to improve groundwater availability.

  • How much water can BoreCharger save?

    BoreCharger can significantly increase water storage in borewells by improving recharge. The amount of water saved depends on the borewell size, local groundwater levels, and recharge capacity, but typically enhances water availability for households and farms.

  • Is BoreCharger suitable for all types of borewells?

    BoreCharger is designed to work with most types of borewells, including domestic, agricultural, and municipal wells. The solution is customized based on the borewell depth, diameter, and local geological conditions.

  • How often should BoreCharger maintenance be done?

    For optimal performance, BoreCharger maintenance is recommended once or twice a year, depending on local water conditions. Regular maintenance ensures effective recharge and sustained water availability.

  • Can BoreCharger help during droughts?

    Yes! BoreCharger enhances groundwater recharge, which increases water availability even during dry seasons and droughts. It is a sustainable solution for water-stressed regions.

  • Is BoreCharger eco-friendly?

    Absolutely! BoreCharger uses sustainable methods to recharge groundwater without harmful chemicals or energy-intensive processes. It supports long-term water conservation and environmental protection.

  • How can I get BoreCharger installed?

    You can contact Urdhvam Environmental Technologies Pvt. Ltd. through their official website or call their support team. They will assess your site and provide a customized BoreCharger installation solution.
